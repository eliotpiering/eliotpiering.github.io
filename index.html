What it does

Clink gives your ownership of your beer by creating a one-to-one connection between your phone and your bottle

If you have ever lost track of your beer at a party, finding it again is as simple as tapping you phone to any bottle and getting visual and audio confirmation if that bottle was your beer.

Klink reduces beer waste. Less wasted beer makes it easier than ever to be #UpForWhatever

How it works

Each bottle has a tiny NFC sticker that your smart phone/watch can connect with. Our app records which bottle was yours and informs you if you got the right one.

Future Applications

Connecting a beer uniquely with smartphone users has endless applications. Klink is a simple prototype using this technology. The most powerful future uses include:

Hook into existing social networks so people can know where there friends are partying

Use geolocation data to inform users about bar hotspots

Capture deep anylytics around customer engagement with the brand; Using this technology ABI can see where people are drinking, with who, at what time, and what they are drinking

Engage millenials with their beer by giving them details about the beer he/she is about to consume; Users could see details about where the beer was manufactured, what the born on date was, and additional fun facts about the type of beer.

Embed messages into another person's beer. Want to give that cute girl your number? Send her a message when you touch bottles.
